Pakis Beach

Karawang, Indonesian

Pachis is a beach with a lot of pine trees with emerald and green waters under Skala Rachoni. It is 7km far from Limenas, it is organized with easy access and parking facilities. The sea has shoal waters making it suitable for kids to have fun.

Suluban Beach

Bali, Indonesiann

Suluban Beach is a small untouched white sand haven with clear water and gentle waves that are great for swimming. Crowding here is moderate. It’s not a secluded spot to escape the crowds, but it’s also nowhere near as busy as neighbouring Padang Padang Beach.

Karimun Java

Jawa Tengah, Indonesian

Karimun Jawa is a paradisaical archipelago of 27 coral-fringed islands in the Java Sea off the Indonesian coast of Central Java. Arriving on the two main islands of Karimun Jawa and Kemujan, you'll be greeted with warm smiles and an assortment of colorful fishing boats.

Drini Beach

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

Drini Beach is one of the spectacular beaches in Gunungkidul Regency, Special Region of Yogyakarta. Known for its stunning beach beauty and tranquil atmosphere, Drini Beach offers a satisfying tourist experience with its beautiful sea panorama and clean sandy shores.

Baron Beach

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

Nice beach with not too huge waves. Not a white sand beach. A bit dirty, but lots of seafood stalls n market. Lots of fruit stalls also.

Trisik Beach

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

Trisik Beach has a speciality compared to other beaches in Kulon Progo, namely the atmosphere of coastal villages that is so pronounced.
